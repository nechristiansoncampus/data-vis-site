## Data Model 

**students**
- firstname (String)
- lastname (String<optional>)
- classOf (String<optional>)

**appointments**
- title (String)
- date (String)
- students (Array<String>)
- fulltimers (Array<String>)
- notes (String<optional>)

**events**
- title (String)
- date (String)
- students (Array<String>)
- fulltimers (Array<String>)
