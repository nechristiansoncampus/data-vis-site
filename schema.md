## Data Model 

**students**
- firstname (String)
- lastname (String: optional)
- classOf (String: optional)

**appointments**
- title (String)
- date (String)
- students ([String])
- fulltimers ([String])
- notes (String: optional)

**events**
- title (String)
- date (String)
- students ([String])
- fulltimers ([String])
